# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 26, 2026**

**Core Principle:** Today's Income is a purely local app. All data is stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

## English Version

### 1. Data Collection

We **do not collect** any user data. All data in this app is stored locally on your device:

- Income records: stored in local database on your device
- Punch records: stored in local database on your device
- User settings: stored in local configuration on your device

We **do not** use any analytics tools, tracking, or third-party SDKs to collect user data.

### 2. Data Storage

All your data (income information, punch records, working hours, etc.) is stored on your iOS device using iOS system standard storage mechanisms. We do not own or access any of your data.

### 3. Biometric Authentication

The app supports Face ID / Touch ID to protect your data within the app. This feature is provided by iOS's native LocalAuthentication framework. Your biometric data is never obtained or stored by us.

### 4. Local Notifications

The app may send you local notifications (such as overtime reminders). These notifications are generated locally on your device by iOS. We do not collect any notification-related data.

### 5. Data Transmission

**This app does not contain any network request functionality.** We will not send any of your data to any server. All information you enter is stored only on your device and will not be transmitted anywhere.

### 6. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, advertisements, or trackers.

### 7. Children's Privacy

We do not collect any personal information, including information from children.

### 8. Privacy Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page. Continuing to use the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
